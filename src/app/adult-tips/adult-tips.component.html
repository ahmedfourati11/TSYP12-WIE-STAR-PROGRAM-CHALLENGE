TIPS

Promote Collaboration:

Support group projects in class and at home, like joining science clubs or team challenges. Collaboration builds essential skills and enhances enthusiasm for STEM.

Make STEM Fun:

Use interactive tools like Kahoot or organize events like science fairs. Fun, gamified learning activities engage students and show that STEM can be enjoyable.

Foster Curiosity:

Encourage students to ask questions and explore how things work. Whether through hands-on experiments in class or fun STEM activities at home, showing enthusiasm for discovery can spark their interest.

Provide Resources:

Share free online courses, competitions, and STEM-related clubs. Access to tools and mentorship can open up opportunities for deeper learning.
