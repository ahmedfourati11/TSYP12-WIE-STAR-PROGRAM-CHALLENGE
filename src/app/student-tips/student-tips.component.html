TIPS

Focus on Fundamentals:

Master core concepts in math and science as they are the foundation for understanding advanced topics.

Practice Regularly:

Solve problems, run experiments, and practice coding frequently to build skills and confidence.

Ask Questions:

Stay curious and seek help from teachers, peers, or online resources whenever a concept is unclear.

Apply What You Learn:

Engage in real-world projects or experiments to see how STEM concepts work in practical scenarios.
